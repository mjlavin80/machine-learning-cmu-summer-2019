I’m an aging baby boomer, raised to believe that good music ended sometime in the 1970s. But around 2010 I discovered that this is anything but true — if anything, the end of the CD era and the shift to live performance as a source of income has brought on a renaissance of incredibly talented musicians. If anything I worry that there’s too much good music out there — how can all of those who deserve to make it? But anyway, I keep discovering new artists who take my breath away.
